DFA Designer

A DFA (also know has a Determinitic Finite State Automation is a common tool which is used to determine whether or not a particular regular language accepts or rejects a string

  • Click the canvas to start
  • Add a state: Either select an accepting state(double circle) or a transition state(cicle) from the items menu and click on canvas to add
  • Add a label to the state: click on text box inside the circle and edit
  • Add a line: click on the line icon from the line menu and click on canvas and drag
  • Delete and item: click on the item and press the delete key on your keyboard
