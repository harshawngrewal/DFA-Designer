DFA Designer

A DFA (also know has a Determinitic Finite State Automation is a common tool which is used to determine whether or not a particular regular language accepts or rejects a string

  • Add a state: Either select an accepting state(double circle) or a transition state(cicle) from the items menu and click on canvas to add.
  • Add a label to the state: Double click the state and edit label
  • Add a line: click on the line icon from the line menu and click on canvas and drag, Or hold shift on keyboard an drag on canvas. To curve the line, adjust the line by clicking and holding the pivot and moving it in desired direction
  • Clear: Click the clear button from the menu to erase entire canvas
  • Eraser: Click on the eraser button from menu and then click on any shape on the canvas to erase that are on the canvas
