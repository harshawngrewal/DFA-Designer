DFA Designer

A DFA (also know has a Determinitic Finite State Automation is a common tool which is used to determine whether or not a particular regular language accepts or rejects a string

  • Click the canvas to start
  • Add a state: Either select an accepting state(double circle) or a transition state(cicle) from the items menu and click on canvas to add. Or double click on an empty spot in the canvas
  • Add a label to the state: Double click the state and edit label
  • Add a line: click on the line icon from the line menu and click on canvas and drag, Or hold shift on keyboard an drag on canvas. To add a curved line either click a state and press L on keyboard or select from menu and add to canvas
  • Delete/Clear: click on the item and press the delete key on your keyboard, or click on the 'CLEAR' button to clear the whole canvas
